Bigg Jah Is A YouTuber Whose Real Name Is Jahdai Pickett

Jahdai Pickett is an American Youtuber who is popular through his stage name, Bigg Jah. 

He is a Los Angeles-based comedian, actor, writer, director, and influencer. Jah got his start behind the camera filming sketches for other comedians, and he now has over 1.46 million subscribers on YouTube and Facebook.

On YouTube alone, he has over 454 million views.

Jah is known for his relatable takes on everyday situations. Many of his episodic sketches, such as Tiberius: The Hood Hitman, When The Whole Crew is Stupid, and TheLesbian Homie, have become popular. 

He takes pride in being a funny filmmaker who creates visual "hood comedy" sketches to which a wide range of audiences can relate.

TheLesbian Homie, a recently concluded ten-episode series is arguably the top fare on his channel. It chronicles a fictional Pickett's comical suffering when, for the first time, he sees his one-of-the-bros lesbianfriend glammed up to go out.

 It has received nearly 40 million views in total. Moreover, he recently released season 2 of the series whose second episode premiered on September 27, 2022. 

You can also watch the full episodes without having to wait if you purchase Replay for around $13.

Later episodes of the show highlight Pickett's clever brand-building in the form of merch from his Hood Good Store.

It sells T-shirts, hoodies, headwear, and other items emblazoned with slogans from his videos that have become popular with fans, such as the "got yams?" shirt. 

Bigg Jah's Net Worth: How Rich Is The YouTuber?

Bigg Jah has an estimated net worth of $635,000 as of 2022. The primary source of his income comes from the comedy sketches that he uploads online. 

Pickett joined the channel on December 14, 2011, and as of now, he has a total of 454,521,990 views. 

He began this journey as a UC Berkeley film degree graduate. Bigg started doing stand-up comedy in Los Angeles in 2009, but he made sketches only around 2010, however, he was inconsistent. 

Bigg accumulated all of his knowledge from writing comedic short sketches to producing them, and the majority of it was extremely consistent.

Since early 2017, he began posting at least two videos per week on Instagram. He released a sketch called "Tiberius: The Hood Hitman," which quickly went viral.

That's when things began to change for him. His followers on all three platforms, viz, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube continue to grow, which led to opportunities and global recognition.

He considers his content to be "Hood Good Comedy," citing Martin Lawrence, Eddie Murphy, Jamie Fox, Spike Lee, and others as influences.

He wishes to resurrect the Urban Comedy that he believes has been lost in the last two decades.

Based on his YouTube subscribers, Social Blade estimated his monthly income to be between $1.8K and $28.5K. 

Similarly, Forbes reported that a YouTuber with exceptional talent can earn $5 for every 1,000 video views. A million views on YouTube can be worth up to $5,000. 

Based on this calculation, he had total career earnings of $2,272,609 as of September 2022, with 454,521,990 views.
