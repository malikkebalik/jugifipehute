Katy Mixon Exclusive Portraits Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Actress Katy Mixon visited HollywoodLife.com to discuss season 4 of her amazing show American Housewife. The ABC sitcom follows Katy Mixon as Katy Otto, the matriarch of the Otto family who resides in wealthy Westport, Connecticut. In its fourth season, the show continues to explore Katy’s handling of her three children, who she tries to keep “as normal as possible” amongst their priveledged and spoiled peers. Katy’s teen, Taylor, is played by Meg Donnelly; her middle son, Oliver, is portrayed by Daniel DiMaggio; and Katy’s youngest (and favorite), Ana-Kat, is hysterically portrayed by Julia Butters. Diedrich Bader stars as Katy’s tenured husband Greg, who is an involved father and husband.
