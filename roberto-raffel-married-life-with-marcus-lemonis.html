Roberto Raffel' Married Life with Marcus Lemonis

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

The perk of getting married to a celebrity is that you become one too. Roberta Bobbi Raffel rose to the limelight after getting married to one of the widely accomplished businessmen, Marcus Lemonis.

At this generation, the age gap in marriage is pretty normal when a man is older, but it was quite opposite this time. Surprisingly, the bride, Roberta Raffel is 20 years older than her actor husband. So, without wasting much time, we take you close to the personal and married life of the celebrity wife.

Roberta Raffel Married to Millionaire Marcus Lemonis

People often say, age is just a number and the duo of Roberta Raffel and Marcus Lemonis just validated it with their relationship. After more than a year of dating, the duo surprised everyone by marrying in February 2018.

The lovely duo exchanged their vows at a fancy ceremony at Hotel Ble-Air in front of close friends and family. Earlier, the actor from The Profit went down on one knee to propose Bobbi on the occasion of Christmas 2017.

How did Roberta Raffel Meet her Soulmate Marcus Lemonis?

Destiny played its part as Raffel happened to meet Marcus – a businessman cum actor in 2016. It wasn’t the most romantic location that they met for the first time as they crossed paths at a trade show.
