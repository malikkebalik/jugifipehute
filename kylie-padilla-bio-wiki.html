Kylie Padilla Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 6, 2024

Who is Kylie Padilla?

Kylie Padilla is an Australian-Filipino actress and singer who was awarded the Best New Female Personality award by PMPC Star awards.

Likely, she is well known for the role of Erenea in the series Joaquin Bordado.

Kylie Padilla: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

She was born in Calatagan, Batangas, Philippines on January 25, 1993. Her birth name is  Kylie Nicole Sicangco Padilla and she is currently 29 years old. Her birth sign is Aquarius.

She was born to her parents; Robin Padilla (father) and Liezl Sicangco (mother).

She has six siblings namely, Queenie Padilla, Zhen-Zhen Padilla, Ali Padilla, Camille Orosa, Maria Isabella Padilla, and Mico Padilla. Kylie holds Philippine- Australian citizenship.

Education

Talking about Kylie’s educational history, she attended Queensland, Australia.

Kylie Padilla: Professional Life and Career

The actress followed in the footstep of her father. During the early stage of her life, she had a contract star for GMA Network. At that time she appeared on television series for GMA including ‘The Good Daughter,’ ‘Joaquin Bordado’ and ‘Adarna.’

Kylie didn’t limit her talents to a certain extent. Some of the other television series she appeared as a star in ‘Are Encantadia,’ ‘Unforgettable,’ ‘And Dwarfina’ to name a few.

Kylie has appeared on a variety of shows as a guest star. She is a passionate girl with a dream to hold a prominent place in showbiz.

Kylie has appeared in ‘Sa Ngalan ng Ama, Ina, At Mga Anak,’ ‘Dilim,’ and ‘Triptiko’ movies.

We can see Kylie and her father in different commercials as she has done various advertisements during her career.

For their performance in different shows and movies, her work is appreciated and awarded. Furthermore, she is a dedicated star who loves to work in the entertainment industry with great enthusiasm.

Awards

She won the Female Face of the Night at Special Award in 2015.

Kylie Padilla: Net Worth, Salary

She has an estimated net worth of around $2 million as of 2022, and she has accumulated that sum of money from her professional career according to the sources.
