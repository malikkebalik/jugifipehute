Georgia May Foote is an English actress, model, dancer, and reality television personality who is known for playing roles like Alison Simmons in the children’s television drama series Grange Hill, Katy Armstrong in the television soap opera Coronation Street, Angela Fairley in the BBC Three crime drama series Conviction, Falista in The CW fantasy adventure drama series The Outpost, Rosie in the 6-part BBC television drama series Sorted, and Holly in the television sitcom Trollied. Additionally, she has also been a part of a variety of other projects which include Life on Mars, Doctors, Casualty, All Star Family Fortunes, Heartbeat, Cotton Stones, Emmerdale, This Is England ’86, Loose Women, Celebrity Juice, Fake Reaction, Strictly Come Dancing, Strictly Come Dancing: It Takes Two, Saturday Kitchen, and Queen of Diamonds.
