Kevin Sussmans Dating Life with Soon-To-Be Wife Addie Elizabeth Hall

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Stuart's days of being sad and single are over!

Kevin Sussman, most known for his portrayal of Stuart Bloom on the hit TV show The Big Bang Theory, is officially engaged. The actor popped the question to his now fiancée, Addie Hall, in March 2022.

Here is a look into their love life, when things started between the two, and if Sussman is planning to make her his new wife anytime soon.

Kevin Sussman Got Engaged to Girlfriend Addie Elizabeth Hall

On March 29, 2022, Sussman surprised everyone on the internet with an engagement announcement as he uploaded a picture with his now fiancée, Hall, on both Instagram and Twitter.

"Folks... she said yes!" the caption of the posts read as he attached a picture of them at a restaurant named Pastis somewhere. She had her left hand over his shoulder, which gave everyone a clear view of her now-ringed finger.

The couple looked elated as they posed for the camera.

Sussman's engagement announcement post with his soon-to-be wife, Addie Elizabeth Hall. (Photo: Kevin Sussman/Instagram)

Both his Instagram and Twitter posts were soon flooded with congratulatory messages for Sussman and Hall.

His on-screen partners Lauren Lapkus and Brian O'Halloran were two of the many who gave their good wishes to the newly engaged couple.

When Did Kevin Sussman Start Dating Addie Elizabeth Hall?

There's no exact date on that available yet as the couple has not revealed much about their relationship. However, they had become comfortable enough with each other and their romance by October 2020.

Sussman made his dating life with Hall Instagram official through a post on October 19, 2020. By the looks of the picture and the caption, the couple probably went pumpkin hunting shortly before the upload.

Since then, the actor has been posting about Hall fairly often in between posts about his dogs, sketches on his iPad, and work-related stuff. They celebrate holidays together and co-parent his furry kids.

In most of his posts, he makes jokes about his relationship and how great a time he is having.

"Had so much fun on the east coast (normally I don't like fun but it was unavoidable," Sussman captioned one of his Instagram posts with her from July 10, 2021, posting a lovely picture of the two in the first slide, and a video of him dancing to a metal tune in the second.

On Valentine's Day 2022, instead of uploading a picture with his then girlfriend, he uploaded one where he and his dog were seen sleeping on his bed. This was the setup of the joke.

The punchline came on February 20 as he put up a selfie with Hall and wrote, "This is to prove to Addie’s mom that we’re still together. (My Valentine’s Day post had her concerned)."

Sussman and Hall made their red carpet debut on February 24, 2022, as they attended the premiere of The Dropout, and called her a "gorgeous creature" in a subsequent post about the same.

Kevin Sussman’s Dating Life before Addie Elizabeth Hall

Before Sussman started dating Hall, he was in a relationship with Whitney Rice. They dated for much of 2018.

Sussman with his ex girlfriend, Whitney Rice. (Photo: Kevin Sussman/Instagram)

2018 was also the year The Big Bang Theory star finally ended his marriage with Alessandra Young.

He reportedly married Young in January 2006 and separated on New Year's Eve in 2012. They lived that way for over six years, when finally, in October 2017, they signed their divorce papers.

The actor settled things with Young with a one-time payment instead of endless spousal support. The payment was done when they first separated and not when the divorce became official in 2018.
