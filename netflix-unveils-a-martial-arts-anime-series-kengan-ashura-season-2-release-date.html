Netflix Unveils A Martial Arts Anime Series Kengan Ashura Season 2 Release Date!!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 9, 2024

Kengan Ashura is an action-packed martial arts anime series that originated as a manga by Yubako Sandrovich. This Netflix Original series, animated by Larx Entertainment, brings to life a thrilling world of gladiatorial combat. With its global distribution through Netflix, audiences worldwide can immerse themselves in this epic tale.

Netflix’s original anime series, Kengan Ashura, has recently revealed the highly anticipated trailer and release date for its second season. Set to premiere on the streaming platform in Fall 2023, fans are eagerly awaiting the continuation of Ohma Tokita’s journey to secure victory for himself and the Nogi Group.

After much speculation, it has been confirmed that the second season of Kengan Ashura will premiere in 2023. The following is the current state of knowledge concerning Kengan Ashura season 2. Dive into this exhilarating world and witness the triumphs, trials, and transformations that await the combatants in their relentless pursuit of victory.

Season 1 of Kengan Ashura Brief

Before we talk about what will happen in Season 2, let’s take a quick look at what happened in Season 1. Through Kazuo Yamashita, an average salaryman who becomes the trainer of a fighter named Ohma Tokita, we learned about Kengan matches. Ohma, who is also known as “Ashura,” is a strong fighter who competes in Kengan matches for the thrill of the fight and to learn more about his past.

Protagonist and Plot

The story follows protagonist Tokita “Ashura” Ohma, who is scouted by the Nogi Group after being discovered by Yamashita Kazuo, a Japanese salaryman working for the organization. Motivated by an insatiable desire to defeat any opponent, Tokita participates in the tournament. However, as the competition progresses, his past begins to resurface, presenting new challenges and revelations.

Background and Manga Series

Kengan Ashura originated as a manga series created by Yabako Sandrovich (author) and Daromeon (illustrator). The series premiered in April 2012 on Shogakukan’s Ura Sunday digital publication website and concluded in August 2018 after 237 chapters were compiled into 27 volumes. A sequel titled Kengan Omega was launched in January 2019.

Synopsis and Setting

Kengan Ashura delves into the underground world of Kengan tournaments, where wealthy business owners and merchants hire gladiators to fight on their behalf, a tradition dating back to the Edo period. These tournaments were used to settle disputes and gain advantages in the business world, particularly within the realm of megacorporations.
