What Happened To April Kauffman? Oxygen True Crime Reveals The Story On Why Her Husband Did It

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

April Kauffman was a beloved radio host in New Jersey, killed by the biker gang under her husband's command.

In May 2012, April Kauffman's murder shocked her followers and the public. She was one of the favorite TV hosts of the people, with her fans admiring her. 

Kauffman was in her late 40s when the tragedy hit. Moreover, the case remained cold for around five years, with no lead on her murder. 

April's murder case came to flash again with the Oxygen revisiting the devastating death of the radio host. Learn more about the terrifying incident with an anchor.

What Happened To April Kauffman? 

April Kauffman, a New Jersey radio host, died after getting shot. A biker gang infamous for the Pagans killed her with her husband's order.

April was found dead in her bedroom when the police officers arrived at the scene. As mentioned in the Oxygen True Crime, she was shot twice to death. 

Following Kauffman's death, the officers couldn't find any lead with no hint of forced entry, stolen items, fingerprints, and no shell casings. It later revealed that the bike gang Pagans were involved in the killing. 

Upon further investigation, the police found out April's husband's motive to kill her. As mentioned in the Oxygen True Crime, they believed that the radio host discovered her husband's truth of double life and threatened him. 

Is April Kauffman On Wikipedia?- Bio Explored

April Kauffman was not available on the Wikipedia page. However, she was a well-known media personality notable for her work as a radio host. 

April was 47-years of age when she died in her bedroom. Besides radio talk shows, she was notable as an entrepreneur & a business owner, also an active volunteer at veteran's efforts.

Kauffman also had prior experience working in WIBG, JAK Purveyors Catering Co, and Artistic Salon.  

April Kauffman Killer Husband Jim Kauffman

April Kauffman was married to her husband Jim Kauffman, a doctor, in 2000. Those two were known as a power couple in the community until the host died tragically. 

Moreover, April's husband, Jim, a doctor, got arrested for his involvement with her murder. He also faced allegations of medical fraud for supplying Oxycontin to the drug ring.

Kauffman's husband, Jim, died in January 2018 by suicide in the prison cell. 

April Kauffman's Net Worth In Insurance Value Given By Daughter Kim

April Kauffman had a daughter named Kimberly Pack. Her husband sold all the possessions without informing her daughter Kim. 

However, April's child Kim hired a lawyer after learning Jim was also eligible for her mother's life insurance policy of $600k.
