Overall Meaning

The song "Jam" by Kevin Gates is a catchy track that showcases his signature flow and storytelling abilities. The first verse has him riding around in his car listening to his own music with a girl. The girl wants to hear track six, which she considers her jam. Gates then goes on to describe their sexual encounter, with him being all she needs and their song probably being played in the background.


In the second verse, Gates describes a different woman who is looking at herself in the mirror, not liking what she sees. Gates then proceeds to reassure her and offer his own version of intimacy while also highlighting that it is their business only. He ends the verse with the line "Hit you with the Ruger, the bitch don't (jam)", which could be interpreted as a violent threat, but it's unclear.


The chorus is repeated between each verse and at the end of the song, with the title referring to a woman's favorite song, and Kevin Gates being all in-between, which she can't get enough of, hence it's probably on (jam).
