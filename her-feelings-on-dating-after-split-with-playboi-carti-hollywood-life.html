Her Feelings On Dating After Split With Playboi Carti Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Iggy Azalea, 30, may be single after breaking up with ex Playboi Carti, 24, but she’s not quite ready to step back into a romance with someone new just yet. The Australian beauty recently revealed that she’s no longer in a relationship with the rapper, who is also the father of her five-month-old son Onyx, in a social media post, and it turns out she’s ready to take this alone time to focus on herself.

“Right now she’s taking a break from men and just focusing on herself but of course she has guys lining up to try and date her,” a source EXCLUSIVELY revealed to HollywoodLife. “But she’s playing it cool for now and just enjoying her time as a new mom.”

In fact, the proud parent is looking forward to the holiday season with her new little tot, who can be seen in the pics below. “She’s so excited about Christmas with Onyx, it’ll be his first and she’ll make it very magical for him,” the source explained. “She still talks to her ex because she wants to make sure he can still see Onyx when he wants to so that’s the only reason they still talk.”

Although Iggy and Playboi had been in a private relationship for around two years, she’s now been embracing life as a single woman and prioritizing Onyx in every way possible. “Iggy is not interested in dating right now,” a second source EXCLUSIVELY shared. “She wants to go through the end of the year and into the holidays with Onyx. No distractions from men.”

The blonde beauty is also ready to work on new music now that she’s no longer involved in a romance. “She is going to work on music but she wants to not worry about dating or men for the rest of the year,” the second source further told us. “Maybe when Onyx gets older but for now she is avoiding any drama that a relationship might bring.”
