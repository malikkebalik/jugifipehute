Luis Castro spent most of his 17-year professional career as a footballer in the lower leagues, representing O Elvas CAD, U.D. Leiria, R.D. Águeda, and AD Fafe in the Segunda Liga and Vitória S.C. and Elvas in the Primeira Liga.

With the latter, he presented in 28 matches in the 1987–88 season, but his team ranked in 15th place and suffered relegation.

In a managerial career of over a quarter of a century, Luis Castro led 4 teams in his nation’s top flight including briefly Porto, where he won the 2nd tier with the reserve team in 2016.

He also won a Ukrainian Premier League title for Shakhtar Donetsk in 2020 and worked in Qatar and Brazil.
