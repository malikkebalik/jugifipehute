Meet Kevin Chamberlin-Theater, Film, and Tv actor known for Disney's sitcom, Jessie.

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Kevin Chamberlin is an American actor known for his work on television and theater. He is recognized for playing theatrical roles such as Horton in Seussical and Uncle Fester in The Addams Family. Kevin appeared as Bertram Winkle in Disney's sitcom Jessie from 2011-2015. Likewise, he starred as The Wizard of Oz in Wicked on Broadway from 2018-2019.

Kevin was born on November 25, 1963, in Baltimore, Maryland, the U.S. He graduated with a BFA degree from Rutgers University, New Brunswick. For his theatre roles, Chamberlin has received three Tony Awards and three Drama Desk Award nominations.
