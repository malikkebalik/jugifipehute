David Silva is a Spanish attacking midfielder who currently plays for Real Sociedad in La Liga. Born on January 1986, the left footed midfielder has also played for Manchester City for 10 long years from 2010-2020. He has 309 appearances for the Citizens with over 60 goals. 

The 2010 Spanish World Cup winner has represented the national team in 125 games with 35 goals scored. He has also won 2 UEFA European Champions for the Spanish national side and 1 for the U-19 team in 2004. David Silva is an extraordinary dribbler with a possession-based gameplay which suits the Pep Guardiola’s style of play.

David Silva has a son, Mateo, born in December 2017. He was born 3 months earlier than expected and was at a risk of losing his life for 5 long months.
