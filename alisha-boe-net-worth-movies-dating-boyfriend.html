Alisha Boe Net Worth, Movies, Dating, Boyfriend

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Alisha Ilhaan Bø, known professionally as Alisha Boe, is a Norwegian-Somali actress. She is known for playing Jessica Davis in the Netflix drama series 13 Reasons Why, which she landed at the age of 19.

Boe made her acting debut in 2008 in the horror film, Amusement as Child Lisa. She gradually made her way up to the Hollywood limelight starring and guest-starring in TV series like Modern Family, Extant, Casual on Hulu, CSI: Cyber, Ray Donovan & Teen Wolf.

Net Worth of Alisha Boe

Alisha Boe today is set to have an estimated net worth of $1 million as of December 2019. Alisha has the credit of more than 20 movies and TV series to her name during her 11-year and ongoing career.

Boe debuted in the movie Amusement in 2008 at the tender age of 11. In the year 2009, she starred in the movie Plastic makes Perfect as Brooke, and He’s on My Mind as Laci.

2012 was the year Alisha made her debut in TV Series Parenthood: Everything is Not Ok as a Pretty Girl. In the same year, Bo starred in the supporting character as Tara in the American supernatural horror film Paranormal Activity 4 (budget $5 million), which grossed around $143 million worldwide.

In 2013, Bo appeared in one episode of the TV series Trophy Wife: Pilot, as Chelsea Trasssen. Likewise, she guest-starred in the single episode of The Modern Family as Tracy and as Brynn Hendy in two episodes of thriller series Extant opposite of Halle Berry in 2014.

2014 was a busy year for Alisa because, apart from the roles mentioned above, she was shooting as the character of Daphne for the TV Series, Days of Our Lives. The following year, she starred as Farrah Meyers in NCIS, Becca in Casual, Grace Clarke in CSI: Cyber, Janet in the Showtime hit series Ray Donovan and Gwen in Teen Wolf.

In 2017, 68 Kills, a black comedy crime film released to great reviews where Alisha starred alongside Matthew Gray Gubler and AnnaLynne McCord. He’s On My Mind, Plastic Makes Perfect!, Paranormal Activity 4 are some of the movies in her acting credits.

After the release of the indie feature film, 68 Kills in 2017, she landed her breakthrough role as Jessica Davis in the Netflix Original series 13 Reasons Why, where she receives around $135,000 per episode along with the co-star, Brandon Flynn, Justin Prentice, Christian Navarro, Miles Heizer, Devin Druid, and Ross Butler.

Alisha Bo Growing Up

Alisha was born in Oslo, Norway, to a Somali father and a Norwegian mother, Vibeke Boe, from Trondheim, Norway. She was seven years old when she moved to Los Angeles after her mother married an American man. She’s got an 11-year older sibling named Asante Pharrell Boe who lives with her mother.

Boe attended Woodlake Elementary School, George Ellery Hale Middle School, Ivy Academia, and graduated middle school at Hale. She took drama program as her major in El Camino Real High School, commonly known as “ECR” or “Elco” located in the Woodland Hills district of the San Fernando Valley region of the city of Los Angeles, California.

The high school Boe went for graduation is also known for alumni actors who also enrolled in their drama program. Alisha attended Olin Virtual Academy for her junior and senior years of High School to balance her studies and work. The 22-year-old is the first Somali actress to hold a leading role in an American film since Iman.

Boe confessed of getting a rose tattoo on her left hand after she broke up with her boyfriend in a game show Never Have I Ever in MTV International Youtube channel on May 19, 2018. Consequently, she was rumored to be dating a guy, Scott O’Brien around May 2017 until she wiped out his picture from her Instagram.

Since then, Boe often shares glimpses of her shows on Instagram. Moreover, she uploads pictures alongside her co-star friends on the photo-sharing platform, which boasts 4.5 million followers. However, there is no sign from the pretty star about her relationship and dating endeavor on the internet. We may see her in a relationship in the near future, but for now, she is enjoying her stardom alone.
