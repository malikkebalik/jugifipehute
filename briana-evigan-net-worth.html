Briana Evigan Wiki Biography

Briana Barbara-Jane Evigan was born on 23 October 1986, in Los Angeles, California USA, of Polish and Italian descent. Briana is an actress and dancer, best known for being a part of the “Step Up” film series, in which she plays Andie West. She was also a part of “Sorority Row”, and has been active in the industry since 1996. All of her efforts have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Briana Evigan? As of early-2018, sources estimate a net worth that is at $8 million, mostly earned through a successful career in acting. She is particularly well-known for appearing in numerous horror and thriller films. As she continues her career, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Briana Evigan Net Worth $8 million

At a young age Briana started studying dance, and also learned to play the keyboard, appearing as one of the singers in the group Moorish Idol. She attended Los Angeles Valley College, and during her time there studied speech and communication.

After her studies, Evigan started working as a professional dancer, appearing in several musical videos, of Enrique Iglesias, Linkin Park and Flo Rida among others. She also started to have small roles on television and in films. In 2008, she won an audition which led her to become a part of the “Step Up” sequel, “Step Up 2: The Streets” – she had a breakthrough performance in the film as although it received negative reviews, it was a box office success, which led her to have more opportunities, increasing her net worth correspondingly. The following year she became a part of the film “S. Darko”, which didn’t receive good reviews unlike the original “Donnie Darko”, but in 2008 Evigan was then cast as a part of the horror film “Sorority Row”, which featured Audrina Patridge and Rumer Willis. The film proved highly successful at the box office, despite a mixed critical reception. Her performance gained praise from the critics, and soon she was cast in more horror/thriller films.

Briana became a part of the remake of “Mother’s Day”, but numerous setbacks led the film to be released late. She also starred in the film “Burning Bright” which found a lot of good reviews, but never received a theatre release. Her next project would be the indie film “Subject: I Love You”, and she then became a part of the short film “The Devil’s Carnival”, before reprising her role in the sequel “The Devil’s Carnival: Alleluia”. In 2014, she reprised her role in “Step Up: All In” before appearing in “Love Is All You Need?” The following year, she announced that she was going to be a part of the second season of “From Dusk Till Dawn”, and another of her latest projects is the short film “Angel of Anywhere”, which was released in 2017.

For her personal life, Briana Evigan’s past romantic relationships include with Robert Hoffman (2007 – 2008) and Patrick John Flueger (2010 – 2013), but she is still single. Her parents are dancer Pamela C. Serpe and actor Greg Evigan. She also has three siblings. She resides in Los Angeles, California.
