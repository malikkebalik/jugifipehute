Zuri Hall is an American entertainment reporter, tv presenter, actress, and producer who has lately made news after losing a significant amount of weight.

Zuri Hall is a reporter for NBC's Access Hollywood. She is also a sideline reporter for American Ninja Warrior, NBC's summer contest program.

In the quest for the next Face of MyINDY-TV, Hall beat hundreds of others to become the first woman to earn the role. She reported local events, starred in station PSAs, and interviewed stars at WNDY-TV in Indianapolis.
