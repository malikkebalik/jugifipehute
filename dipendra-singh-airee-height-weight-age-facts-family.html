Dipendra Singh Airee is a Nepalese cricketer who was one of eleven cricketers chosen to play for Nepal’s first-ever One Day International (ODI) match, against the Netherlands. He also holds the record for the fastest fifty runs made in T20I cricket.

Dipendra Singh Airee Facts

  • He was born and raised in Nepal.
  • On March 11, 2017, Dipendra played his debut List A match for Nepal against Kenya at the 2015–17 ICC World Cricket League Championship. Later, in January 2018, he was named to Nepal’s team at the 2018 ICC World Cricket League Division Two tournament.
  • Dipendra was chosen to be part of Nepal’s squad for their One Day International (ODI) series against the Netherlands in July 2018.
  • During the 19th Asian Games in Hangzhou, Dipendra broke Indian cricketer Yuvraj Singh‘s previously set record of making the fastest fifty in T20I cricket. Airee managed to attain the feat off just 9 balls against Mongolia on September 27, 2023.
