What would you do when the sexiest woman on earth says she likes you? Actually, this happened to actor Geoffrey Arend on a set of a movie and he went on to marry the highly celebrated woman.

So, who is the wife of Geoffrey Arend and are they still together? Moreover, do they share any child of their own? If these are your questions, well, we have the answers below with further details on the career of Arend.

Who is Geoffrey Arend?

Geoffrey Arend is an American actor of films, television, and theater as well as a voice actor. He has been working as a voice actor and actor for more than two decades since 1997.

The 6 feet 3 inches tall actor was born on 28 February 1978 in Manhattan, New York. Arend’s father is American who belonged to German and English descent while his mother is Pakistani. In 1996, he graduated from  Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts in New York.

Geoffrey Arend Married an Actress; Who is His Wife?

Geoffrey Arend is a married man and his wife is none other than actress Christina Hendricks. Arend’s spouse, Hendricks is an American actress and former model who rose to prominence after being named ‘the sexiest woman in the world’ by Esquire and Best Looking Woman in America, both in 2010.

Arend and his life partner, Hendricks tied the knot on 11 October 2009. The duo was introduced to each other in 2007 by their co-star Vincent Kartheiser on the set of Mad Men.

Geoffrey also featured in Natalie Portman-starring movie, Garden State (2004). The comedy-drama earned $35.8 million against $2.5 million budget. Likewise, Devil (2010) earned $62.6 million while Bubble Boy (2001) collected $18 million.
