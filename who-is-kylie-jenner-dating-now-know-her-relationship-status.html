Who is Kylie Jenner Dating Now? Know Her Relationship Status

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 9, 2024

One of the popular media personalities Kylie Jenner has become one of the most talked-about realities here. She is famous for starring in the reality TV series, Keeping Up with the Kardashians since 2007 on E!.

Kylie, who has 248 million Instagram followers, has mainly kept her dating life under wraps or out of the spotlight. Regardless, her pregnancy became one of the most well-guarded secrets. She revealed the news after welcoming her only kid, Stormi Webster, born on February 1, 2018, with her on-again, off-again lover, Travis Scott.

Jenner's admirers are now eager to learn whether she has found a new man or is still seeing Scott. Let's have a look.

Does Kylie Jenner Have a New Man?

The 23 years old Kylie Jenner seems to be single as of now. But in fact, she has not openly spoken about her relationship status. So it is still not known whether she has a new beau or not. 

The beauty mogul mostly give glimpses of her ex-boyfriend Travis and their 3-year-old daughter Stormi to her fans. Moreover, the family of three spends most of their time together, as evidenced through social media.

At the same time, Kylie and Travis have decided to put their romance on hold in order to focus on their respective careers back in 2019. However, it appears as the two have reconciled as of now.

Despite this, the couple has a solid co-parenting relationship with their daughter.

Are Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Back Again?

One of the famous former couples Kylie, 23, and Travis, 29, seem to back in the market again. The couple who separated in October 2019, after two years of dating, is currently seen spending time and messing around together. 

Neverthless, they have always had strong chemistry for around four years and are now exploring their love life romantically again. They have still feelings for each other too and have been co-parenting so well together, the source said.

Not to mention, everyone around them can see that their bond. Ever since their split in 2019, the couple first time attended the 72nd annual Parsons Benefit in New York City with their daughter Stormi. On June 16, the Kylie Cosmetic' owner shared a photo with Travis on Instagram, writing, " 24 hours in NYC".

For now, neither Jenner nor Scott has publically spoken about their new dating life. Besides that, one of her famous dating life that she has been involved in is with Rapper Tyga (2014-2015; 2015-2017).
