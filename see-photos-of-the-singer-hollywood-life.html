Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Stunning and 60! Celebrate singer-songwriter Sheryl Crow’s birthday with a collection of her most stylish photos.

Sheryl Crow is a laid-back singer-songwriter with a knack for crafting mellow pop hits. Born February 11, 1962, the Kennett, Missouri native, has released 10 studio albums, penning hits like “All I Wanna Do”, “If It Makes You Happy”, and “My Favorite Mistake.” She has nine Grammy awards under her belt and has sold over 50 million albums worldwide. The star has also dabbled in acting, appearing in shows like ’30 Rock’, ‘Cougar Town’, ‘One Tree Hill’, and ‘NCIS: New Orleans’.

As for her personal life, Sheryl has enjoyed high-profile romances with musician Eric Clapton, actor Owen Wilson, and pro-cyclist Lance Armstrong. She is also one tough cookie, having survived breast cancer in the mid-2000s.
