How did Craig Chamberlin die ? Know Everything About His Death

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 29, 2024

Craig Chamberlin, a former deputy for the Spokane County sheriff, who served 
Sheriff’s Office for decades, passed away unfortunately on April 30, 2023, at 52 years of age. His sudden passing has left everyone known to him, including his colleagues and especially his family, in deep pain as they mourn the death of Craig Chamberlin. Keep reading this article to learn more about the late Craig Chamberlin, how he died, and much more!

Who was Craig Chamberlin? 

Craig Chamberlin was a former deputy for the Spokane County sheriff who served
Sheriff’s Office for decades. The former
deputy was also a one-time candidate for Spokane County sheriff.

Craig was known and loved by so many, and he was also a highly respected name among his colleagues. He was a dedicated worker, but he was also a family man. His family included his three daughters, Courtney, Makenzie, and Carlee.

How did Craig Chamberlin die?

According to reports, a former deputy for the Spokane County sheriff, Craig Chamberlin, passed away on Sunday evening, April 30, 2023, at 52 years of age. The news of Craig Chamberlin’s death was confirmed by his daughters, who described Craig as their best friend and superhero. Further information about Craig Chamberlin’s cause of death and funeral ceremony arrangements will be released soon.

Fundraiser organised over Craig Chamberlin’s Death 

Following Craig Chamberlin’s unfortunate death, a GoFundMe fundraiser has been organised by Courtney Chamberlin to raise donations for Craig’s funeral ceremony. The fundraiser has a donation goal of $35,000, out of which about $30,000 has already been raised through donations from 330+ donors.

Tributes pour in for Craig Chamberlin

Expressing their condolences over Craig Chamberlin’s death, SERVPRO’s of NW Spokane/ S&W Spokane/ Spokane Valley, shared a tweet on their Twitter handle, @ServpronwS. The tweet reads, “We are heartbroken at the unexpected loss of our colleague and friend, retired Deputy Craig Chamberlin, who passed away suddenly last night, April 30th. Craig was a valued and beloved member of the SERVPRO family, and we will all miss him more than words can express.”

We express our deepest condolences to the family members, friends, and all loved ones of the late Craig Chamberlin. His death is a massive loss for his family, and we pray to the Almighty to give them the strength to pass through this difficult time. May God let Craig’s soul rest easy on the wings of eternity.
